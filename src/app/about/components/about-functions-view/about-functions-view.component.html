My Players

On this page, you can create your players and modify them whenever you want.

To start, click the button new player and set the name and his positions. It then creates a player with default age of 18 and all stats set to 100. Also, his main position gets set to the first of his learned positions

Click edit on the player to adjust his age and skill values and confirm with the check icon. You can also delete created players.

Main position is the position your player is effectively playing on the field. So if a player has roles DC and DL, but you mostly play him as DL, set that to his main position. It will then display the white skills correctly without involving the ones from the other positions.

Effective % is the normalized strength considering only white skills of his main position. These effective white skills are the white ones.

White skill % is the normalized strength considering all white skills. This can differ as soon as a player has more than 1 role. These additional skills are displayed slightly gray.

Top Eleven % is the strength displayed in Top Eleven itself, where all skills are considered.
